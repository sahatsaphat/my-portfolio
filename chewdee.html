The Problem

1 in 6 Thai children (aged 6–48 months) suffers from constipation. 54% of Thai preschoolers are picky eaters who avoid proper meals. The first 3 years are critical for immunity — poor gut health in this window has lasting consequences.

The Solution

ChewDee is a premium baked pop rice snack enriched with CMUB-N14 Thai-Specific probiotic strain and Inulin (prebiotic). Using Microencapsulation + Post-Baking Coating technology to ensure live probiotics survive heat. High Protein / Low Sodium / Vitamin E. Fun poppable shapes kids can hold.

About the Design

The ChewDee packaging was designed to appeal simultaneously to both parents (trust signals: nutrition facts, probiotics, Thai-certified ingredients) and children (cute pop rice character mascots, vibrant teal + yellow palette, playful typography).

As Creative Lead and CEO, I directed the entire visual language — from character mascot design to pitch deck layout — ensuring every touchpoint communicated safety, fun, and science.
