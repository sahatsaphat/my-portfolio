Mission

To transform a traditional fine arts exhibition into a high-traffic, engaging event. The primary objective was to drive attendance, orchestrate a seamless visitor experience, and successfully bridge the gap between profound conceptual art and accessible entertainment.

About the Project

Dissolve/Contain explores the complex dynamics of localized social issues, subtly questioning the true efficacy of top-down problem-solving in micro-communities.

As Creative Event Director, I engineered a comprehensive event strategy: an impactful opening ceremony, two insightful panel discussions, a hands-on creative workshop I personally directed, and a massive closing concert featuring a 7-act lineup with live bands, DJs, and immersive Visual Art & Sound performances.

I spearheaded the physical stage design and led end-to-end on-site management, coordinating technical crews including Sound Engineers and Lighting teams.
