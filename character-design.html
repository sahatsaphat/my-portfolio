About the Project

Driven by the desire to bring a fresh and modern perspective to Chiang Mai University's internal sports event, this project introduces a mascot character inspired by global sporting festivals.

The design draws from CMU's iconic "White Elephant," representing all "Luk Chang" (students) uniting for the games, while the raised torch pays homage to the university's official emblem. The character is stylized to be cute, vibrant, and highly approachable, serving as the spirited face of the competition.

The character was deployed across Instagram and Facebook feeds, bringing a consistent and energetic visual identity to the university's sports communication.

Character Variants

The mascot was adapted into 4 sport-specific variants: Basketball, Football, Badminton, and Swimming — each maintaining the core character while adding sport-appropriate accessories and poses.
