About the Project

KURA TEA (クら) is a matcha beverage brand concept that fuses Japanese minimalism with a quiet, soulful character. The logotype was custom-designed to blend Roman letterforms with subtle Japanese stroke references — the distinctive "k", "u", "r", "a" characters were individually crafted to evoke the aesthetic of a traditional seal while remaining contemporary and readable.

The brand tagline "Matcha and Soul." is both a product descriptor and a philosophical positioning — matcha as ritual, as mindfulness, as daily soul nourishment. Packaging design extends into tote bags and beverage vessels.
