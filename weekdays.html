About the Project

Weekdays© (Garments Youth) is a youth streetwear brand that challenges the mundane repetition of daily life. The identity system was built around a bold, industrial typeface with rounded elements that reference both blueprint grids and circular motion — symbolizing the endless weekday cycle.

The first drop "Conditioned to Fail" explored the Buddhist concept of Sabbe Sankhara Anicca — all conditioned things are impermanent. The campaign artwork visualized this through typography, apparel mockups, and billboard-ready visuals positioned in transit spaces.
